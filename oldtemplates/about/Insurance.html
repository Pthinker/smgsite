{# TITLE: Insurance #}

Summit Medical Group has developed a sophisticated billing process with the flexibility needed to cooperate with any payer or healthcare plan. Summit Medical Group has direct contracts with a diverse group of health plans. Summit Medical Group also accepts assignment of benefit from a variety of others. We will research every option available that will enable us to provide quality healthcare in an efficient, cost-effective manner. In addition, our Patient Services Representatives offer expert assistance in helping you to better understand healthcare plans. To speak with a trained Patient Services Representative, please contact us at (908) 790- 6500.
