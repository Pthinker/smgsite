Experience a unique approach to wellness at Summit Medical Group that goes beyond the doctor's office visit. Apogée Spa offers a comprehensive strategy to support your well being and individualized needs by offering customized therapeutic services in a serene setting within the medical practice.

You do not have to be a patient of Summit Medical Group to access our services, but if you are our highly skilled staff will work with your physician to develop a comprehensive plan to address your needs and ensure the most beneficial results.

  • Fully integrated blend of traditional and complementary treatment options
  • Comprehensive selection of therapeutic massage, including clinical, sports, post surgical, deep tissue, Asian fusion, hot stone, reflexology, and aromatherapy manicures, pedicures, and medicures featuing natural nail care products
  • Hair removal with advanced soy-based product line
  • Head and Sinus treatment
  • Yoga
  • Stress reduction training
  • Exclusive retail products
  • Medical esthetics
