{# TITLE: Reza Momeni, MD #}

Dr. Momeni is a board-certified plastic surgeon whose practice style is based on respect, honesty, and integrity.

Dr. Momeni specializes in cosmetic surgery, hand surgery, and reconstructive surgery of breast and skin.. Besides having given numerous talks on plastic surgery, Dr. Momeni has received multiple awards for his work and published widely in the scientific literature.

He travels internationally to participate in pro bono surgical missions for babies born with facial deformities. Most recently, Dr. Momeni was voted in New Jersey Monthly magazine as a "Top Doc" by his fellow physicians.

Dr. Momeni trained under several recognized plastic surgery leaders, continues to teach students and residents, and gives educational presentations to other physicians.
