{# TITLE: Insurance and Financing #}

INSURANCE PLANS

Non-cosmetic procedures are usually covered by insurance plans if medically necessary. We participate with most insurance carriers (click here for an up-do-date list). For patients whose insurance plans do not participate with us, payment is due at the time of service. Our staff will provide the forms you need to be reimbursed from your carrier.

Cosmetic procedures and treatments are not medically necessary, with rare exceptions. We offer several convenient ways of paying for elective services.

CASH OR CHECK

Payment is due at pre-surgical visit, 2 weeks prior to surgery.

CREDIT CARDS

We accept major credit cards 2 weeks prior to surgery.

MONTHLY PAYMENT PLAN

Flexible payment option is offered by the Plastic Surgery Center through Capital One. It is specifically designed for cosmetic surgery.

  • No initial payment
  • No pre-payment penalty
  • Low, fixed rates
  • Low monthly payments
  • First Payment Not Due for 4-6 weeks
  • Terms up to 60 months
