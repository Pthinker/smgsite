{# TITLE: BOTOX(r) Cosmetic Injection : Botulinium Toxin #}

Botox® is an exciting new option in facial rejuvenation. Botox is a medical compound that can be injected into certain facial muscles in order to weaken and selectively paralyze them. This results in the diminution of wrinkles over the treated area. When used in small doses this procedure is very safe and painless. It can be perfumed in the office. The results are not permanent and last on average for 3-4 months. The procedure can be repeated. The areas that respond best to botox injections are forehead wrinkles and wrinkles around the eyes (also known as the Crow's feet).
