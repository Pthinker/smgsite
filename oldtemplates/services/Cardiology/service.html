Cardiology at Summit Medical Group deals with disorders of the heart and blood vessels, including congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, heart failure, valvular heart disease and electrophysiology.

To make an appointment or call: (908) 273-4300
Location: 1 Diamond Hill Road, Bensley Pavilion - Second Floor

Specialized Services

  • A Heart Failure Clinic to help you stay active, and avoid emergency visits to see your doctor
  • Advanced Heart Failure Treatment options for severe cardiac cases
  • A Coumadin Care Center that helps you manage oral anticoagulation therapy
  • A Pacemaker Center that keeps pacemakers functioning at their best
  • A Lipid Clinic focused on managing blood cholesterol levels

Stay On Top Of Your Heart Health

  • CT Angiography: Medical imaging process for diagnosing blockages in the arteries
  • Calcium Scoring: A fast and simple measurement of plaque buildup inside your coronary arteries
  • Echocardiography: An ultrasound-based diagnostic technique for many heart health concerns
  • Stress Testing (Echo and Nuclear): Tests that measure your heart's response to physical exertion
